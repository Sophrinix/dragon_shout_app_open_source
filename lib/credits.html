About

Built by Rick Blalock.
Co-Designed with Matt Congrove.

Dragon Shout App is an app built to bring users of Skyrim® together. Dragon Shout App is not affiliated with or endorsed by the developers of Skyrim® or Bethesda Game Studios®.

Dragon Shout App is built on top of Appcelerator's Titanium, Parse.com's data services, and pubnub.com's real-time push services.

Special thanks to GameBanshee.com for usage of the awesome alternate map in Dragon Shout App. Head on over there to check out more great resources, walkthroughs, maps, etc.

Privacy Statement

The App uses a registration / login system in order to keep track of markers and journal entries. Registration requires an email only for password recovery purposes and for association with your gravatar.com profile image. Users' information will never be intentionally given out to 3rd party services or companies without prior permission from the user.
